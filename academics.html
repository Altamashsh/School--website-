Academics

A curriculum that grows with every student

From Junior Kindergarten through Grade 12, each level builds on the last.

Junior School · JK–Grade 5

Our youngest students learn through play, exploration, and a structured introduction to literacy and numeracy. Homeroom teachers stay with each class for the full day, building strong relationships and a consistent routine.

Specialist teachers lead French, music, art, and physical education, giving children a well-rounded weekly schedule from their very first year.

Core subjects

  • Language & Literacy
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Discovery
  • Social Studies
  • French
  • Music
  • Visual Arts
  • Physical Education

Middle School · Grades 6–8

Middle School is where students begin moving between subject-specialist classrooms, building the organisational skills and independence they'll need in Senior School.

An advisory programme pairs each student with a faculty mentor who tracks their academic progress and personal growth across all three years.

Core subjects

  • English Language Arts
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Geography & History
  • French or Spanish
  • Drama
  • Design & Technology
  • Health & Physical Education

Senior School · Grades 9–12

Senior School prepares students for university through a broad base of required courses and an expanding range of electives, including Advanced Placement options in Grades 11 and 12.

Dedicated university counsellors begin working with students in Grade 10, supporting course selection, applications, and scholarship searches through graduation.

Subjects & pathways

  • English Literature
  • Calculus & Advanced Math
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • World History & Economics
  • Modern Languages
  • Computer Science
  • Visual & Performing Arts
  • AP Electives (Grades 11–12)
