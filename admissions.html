How It Works

Our admissions process

Five steps from first inquiry to your child's first day.

1. Inquire

Submit an inquiry form or contact our admissions office to request information and arrange a campus visit.

2. Tour & Apply

Visit campus for a guided tour, then submit your formal application along with school transcripts.

3. Assessment

Students complete an age-appropriate assessment and an informal interview with a member of our faculty.

4. Decision & Enrolment

Families receive an admissions decision within two weeks, followed by enrolment paperwork and orientation details.

Tuition & Fees

Tuition varies by grade level and includes textbooks, technology fees, and most field trips. Need-based financial assistance is available for qualifying families — about 18% of our students currently receive support.
