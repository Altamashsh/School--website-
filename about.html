About Us

Our Story

Six decades of education built on curiosity, character, and community.

Since 1962

Founded on a simple idea

Maple Ridge Academy was founded by a small group of teachers who believed that students learn best when they're known — not just by name, but by how they think, what excites them, and where they need support.

More than sixty years later, that idea still shapes our school. We've grown from a single building with 80 students to a full JK–12 campus serving nearly 700, but our class sizes, our attention to individual students, and our sense of community haven't changed.
